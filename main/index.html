About

Gnoke GeoCompass · v1.0

🧭 Gnoke GeoCompass

A GPS-powered compass that calculates bearing and distance to any destination on Earth. Search cities, enter coordinates manually, or pick from presets.

Point your device toward your destination and navigate with confidence. Your data never leaves the device.

⚙️ Under The Hood

📖 How to Use

1. Enable Compass — tap the Enable button to start device orientation tracking (required for live heading).

2. Set Destination — use Navigate → coordinates, a preset city, or Search to find any place on Earth.

3. Navigate — the compass shows your current heading, and the gold arrow + readout shows bearing and distance to your destination.

Tip: Works best outdoors with a clear view of the sky for GPS accuracy.
